# Privacy Policy for Amharic Psalms App

**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

## Introduction

Semayawi Digitals ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Amharic Psalms mobile application ("App," "Service").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

### 2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information that does not directly identify you, including:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App, including features used and time spent
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, and access times

### 3. User Preferences and Settings

The App stores the following information locally on your device to enhance your user experience:

- **Theme Preferences**: Selected theme mode (light/dark/system)
- **Language Settings**: Preferred language selection
- **Font Customization**: Font size and font family preferences
- **Reading Progress**: Last read chapter/bookmark information
- **Reading Streak**: Daily reading streak data
- **Bookmark Data**: Chapters and verses you have bookmarked

**Important**: All this information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers or any external service.
